Mill Building Guide

Welcome to the complete guide to building the Mill in Tiny Terra Spaces. This page covers everything you need to know about designing, placing, and optimizing your mill.

Why Build a Mill?

The Mill is one of the most important structures in Tiny Terra Spaces. It provides essential functionality for your medieval island community and adds to the overall aesthetic of your settlement.

Building Requirements

  • Materials: Stone, Wood, and specialized resources
  • Unlock: Progress through the game to unlock this building
  • Placement: Needs a flat area on your island

Design Tips

When building your mill, consider these design tips to make it look great:

  • Place it near complementary buildings for a cohesive village layout
  • Use pathways to connect it to other structures
  • Add decorative elements around it for visual appeal
  • Consider the terrain elevation for the best visual effect
